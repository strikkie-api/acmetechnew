Overview

If you're experiencing connectivity issues or need to restore your router to its factory settings, follow the steps below to reset your Acme WaveLink S1. This can help resolve network connection problems and is often a useful first step when troubleshooting Wi-Fi issues. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

When Should You Reset?

  • Devices can't connect to the network
  • You're setting up the router for a new location
  • You're troubleshooting with customer support

Reset Options

Option 1: Soft Reset (Reboot Only)

This simply restarts the router without changing any saved settings:

  1. Unplug all cables from the back of the router.
  2. Wait 10 seconds.
  3. Plug the power cable back in first, then reconnect the LAN cable.
  4. Wait up to 2 minutes for the router to fully restart. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Option 2: Factory Reset (Full Reset)

This erases all custom settings and restores the router to default credentials:

  1. Find the Reset button on the back of your WaveLink S1 ‚Äî it's a small pinhole labeled ‚ÄúReset.‚Äù
  2. Use a paperclip or pin to press and hold the Reset button.
  3. Hold for 10 seconds until the power LED starts blinking.
  4. Release the button and wait. The router will reboot automatically.
  5. Once the LEDs stabilize, the reset is complete. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
